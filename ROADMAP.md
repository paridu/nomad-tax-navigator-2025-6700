# Implementation Roadmap 2024-2026

## Phase 1: Foundation (Q3 - Q4 2024)
*   **DTA Knowledge Graph:** Digitizing 500+ bilateral tax treaties into a queryable graph database.
*   **Residency Engine MVP:** Logic for the "183-day rule" and "Center of Vital Interests" across top 20 nomad hubs.
*   **User Portal:** Basic interface for manual entry of travel history.

## Phase 2: Integration & Automation (Q1 - Q2 2025)
*   **Geo-Fencing Logic:** Automated tracking and alerting when approaching tax residency thresholds.
*   **Bank/Payroll Integration:** Connecting to platforms like Deel, Remote, and Wise to reconcile income with local tax rules.
*   **AI Legal Copilot:** LLM-powered interface to explain specific treaty clauses (e.g., Article 15 of OECD model).

## Phase 3: Global Ecosystem (Q3 2025 - 2026)
*   **One-Click Filing:** Generating localized tax returns based on automated data.
*   **B2B Enterprise Suite:** Compliance dashboard for HR departments managing remote global teams.
*   **Government API:** Direct digital filing pipelines with tax authorities (Revenue Departments).
